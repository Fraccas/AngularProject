What is CryptoIO?

CryptoIO is a free to play mobile app where users can work to win crypto rewards by climbing the leaderboard. Players win from a prize pool based on the revenue gained through ads and in-app purchases.

How to Play

  1. Download the mobile app on the Apple App Store or Google Play
  2. Play as a guest, or create a username/ADA crypto wallet address combo.
  3. Rank your way up the leaderboard to win the weekly reward.
